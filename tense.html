Present Simple VS Present continuous

  1. I drink coffee every morning. 习惯。说话的时候可能没有喝。
  2. I am drinking coffee right now. 正在发生，可能不喜欢喝，没有天天喝。只表示当下

Present Continuous / Present Perfect Continuous

I am doning => right now, what's happening
i have been doing => since or for time. the point is the time

I'm working here for 2 years [X]

过去完成时

有两个动词，都是发生在过去。用完成时可以表示哪个先发生。

过去完成进行时

有点像过去完成时，只是这个过去的动作仍然在继续进行。

现在完成进行 VS 现在完成

  • I have been doing VS I have done

I have read the novel. 我看过那本书了. (已看完)(现在完成)
I have been reading the novel. (一直在看,不一定看完)(现在完成进行时)


现在完成时强调已经完成的数量。现在完成进行时强调的是时间一直延续到现在。




  1. She has written many books. VS She has been writing many books.[X]
  2. It has snowed twice this week. VS It has been snowing twice this week.[X]
  3. He has been sleeping *since 9 o'clock. VS He has slept since 9 o'clock'[X] Because the action is going on.is still in progress.He is sleeping
  4. I have been waiting for half an hour. VS I have waited for half an hour.[X] This action is not complete yet.


现在完成时重点是动词的动作已经完成
