MyBatisLog无法打印的原因

在项目的application.yml里面添加如下：
mybatis:

basepackage: com.github.wxiaoqi.security.admin.modules.mapper xmlLocation: classpath:mapper/*/.xml mapper-locations: "classpath*:mapper/*.xml" configuration: log-impl: org.apache.ibatis.logging.stdout.StdOutImpl

然后重启项目
