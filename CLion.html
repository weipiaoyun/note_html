添加Qt Designer插件

File->Settings->Tools->External Tools->点击加号。参数如下
Name: Qt Designer
Tool settings:

Program: C:\Qt\4.8.7\bin\designer.exe Parameters: \(FileName\) (前后加$) Working directory: \(ProjectFileDir\) (前后加$)

cmake-build-debug可以忽略

忽略后重新可生成。
File->Setings->Build,Excution,Deployment->CMake。重新更新即可。先把debug改为release，然后release改为debug。
