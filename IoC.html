Inversion of Control

  • IoC is a Design Principle
  • recommends the inversion of different kinds of controls in object-oriented design to achieve loose coupling between application classes

假设你开车去你工作的地方。这意味着，你控制这辆车。IoC Principle 建议你invert the control，你雇一个出租车司机替换你开车。其它人开车。这就叫Inversion of control ( from you to the cab driver)，你不用开这个车，只需专注主要的工作。
