Present simple

第三人称单数动词要加s或es。其他都是动词原型。

表示：

  • Habits and routines
  • I drink coffee every morning.
  • Shawn goes to the beach on Sundays. (表示的是日常，基本每个星期天都去)
  • Children often play video games after school.
  • Facts
  • Cathy works as a teacher.
  • You sing very well
  • Water boils at 100C
